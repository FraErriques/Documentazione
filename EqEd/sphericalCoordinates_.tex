\section*{Spherical Coordinates}
 	
	Given the spherical coordinates:
	
	\[
	x = r \cos(t) \cos(f)
	\]
	
	\[
	y = r \sin(t) \cos(f)
	\]
	
	\[
	z = r \sin(f)
	\]
	
	The inverse relationships can be expressed as:
	
	\begin{align*}
		r &= \sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2} \\
		f &= \arcsin\left(\frac{z}{r}\right) \\
		t &= \arctan\left(\frac{y}{x}\right)
	\end{align*}
